Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Silence – Do Nothing respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information Collection

We collect limited information to provide and improve the app experience:

  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your device
  • Streak Data: Your consecutive days of app usage
  • Days Away: Days since your last app interaction
  • Usage Analytics: App behavior and interaction patterns via Firebase Analytics

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or location unless you voluntarily provide it.

Usage Data

We use Firebase Analytics to track user behavior and app interactions. This helps us understand how users engage with the app and improve the experience. Analytics data may include:

  • App opens and session duration
  • Feature usage and navigation patterns
  • Device type and operating system
  • General location (country/region level)

Data Storage

User data including User ID, streaks, and days away is stored securely in Firebase Firestore, a cloud-based database service provided by Google. This data is used solely to maintain your app progress and provide personalized features.

Your data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: For tracking user behavior and app performance
  • Firebase Firestore: For storing user data (User ID, streaks, days away)

These services are provided by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Learn more at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

Silence – Do Nothing does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
